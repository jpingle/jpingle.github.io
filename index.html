Introduction:
Jonathan Pingle is a fourth-year undergraduate at Columbia University double majoring in Economics-Political Science and History. In history, he concentrates on Soviet foreign policy towards the Middle East but -quite sincerely- finds everything interesting. He has worked at the International Rescue Committee, the Institute of International Finance, and Caritas Lebanon. He finds writing this introduction in the third-person exceedingly uncomfortable and hopes that the viewer will forgive him for unfortunately stumbling across it. For more on Jonathan please go to the about page.
